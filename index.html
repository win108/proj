The Warzone Timeline

Trace the historical milestones of the epic clash, from the initial invasion to the modern titan battles.

Phase 1: Episode 1-10

The Porcelain Invasion

Skibidi Toilets emerge mysteriously in metropolitan centers, catching humanity off guard. Conventional defenses crumble as their hypnotic singing spreads the toilet virus, converting civilians.

Phase 2: Episode 11-25

Rise of the Alliance

The Cameramen Faction forms, organizing a cybernetic resistance force. Armed with specialized lenses and heavy weaponry, they push back and establish secure zones, capturing toilets for study.

Phase 3: Episode 26-50

Titan Warfare

The conflict escalates with the deployment of Titan units. G-Man Toilet commands armored forces, while the Alliance deploys Titan Cameraman and Titan Speakerman. Mega battles destroy entire city districts.

Phase 4: Episode 51+

The Hypnotic Grid

TV Men debut, introducing teleportation and projection technologies. G-Man Toilet upgrades to cybernetic armor with dual weapon mounts, triggering the current active, high-intensity deadlock.
